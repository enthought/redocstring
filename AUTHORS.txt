Ioannis Tziakos is the main developer and maintainer of the sectiondoc sphinx
extension.

Historical notes:
-----------------

The refactor_doc (original name of sectiondoc) extention started while working
on the Enaml project with Chris Colbert, Robert Kern, Corran Webster, Tim Diller,
and David Wyde at Enthought.

Many people at Enthought have provided feedback, given suggestions and
fixes.
